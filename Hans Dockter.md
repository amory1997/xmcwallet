# Hans Dockter Profile

## Basic Information
- Hans Dockter is the founder and Chief Executive Officer (CEO) of Gradle.
- Nationality: German, currently based in the San Francisco Bay Area, USA.
- Education: University of Göttingen, Germany.

## Career
- Started developing Gradle in 2007, with the first release in 2008.
- Founder and CEO of Gradle Inc., leading the team to make Gradle a global mainstream build automation tool.
- Before founding Gradle, he worked as a software architect and development consultant for several companies, with extensive experience in enterprise software development and project management.
- Active in the international software development community and a frequent speaker at technology conferences.

## Major Achievements
- Designed and led the Gradle project, which has become a mainstream build tool in the Java, Kotlin, and Android ecosystems.
- Promoted enterprise products such as Gradle Enterprise (Develocity), serving world-renowned companies like Netflix and LinkedIn.
- Gradle is known for its plugin system, flexibility, and high performance, and is widely used in CI, DevOps, and automated testing.

## Other Contributions
- Gradle is open source under the Apache License 2.0.
- The name "Gradle" was originally inspired by "Cradle"; for uniqueness and to reflect its connection with the Groovy language, it was changed to "Gradle".
- Involved in promoting Declarative Gradle, Developer Productivity Engineering (DPE), and other advanced concepts.
